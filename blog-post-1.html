Planning a wedding is an exciting yet daunting task. From selecting the perfect venue to choosing the right menu, every detail counts. In this guide, we'll walk you through the essential steps to plan a wedding that reflects your unique love story.

1. Set a Budget

Determine how much you're willing to spend and allocate funds to different aspects of the wedding accordingly. This will help you make informed decisions and avoid overspending.

2. Choose a Date and Venue

Decide on a date that is meaningful to you and your partner. Then, select a venue that suits your style and accommodates your guest list comfortably.

3. Select Your Vendors

Research and book reputable vendors for catering, photography, entertainment, and decoration. Ensure they understand your vision and can deliver accordingly.

4. Create the Guest List

Compile a list of guests you wish to invite. Consider the capacity of your venue and your budget while finalizing the list.

5. Plan the Details

From invitations and attire to favors and transportation, plan all the finer details to ensure a seamless experience for you and your guests.

Remember, this is your special day. Don't be afraid to personalize your wedding to make it truly unique and memorable.
